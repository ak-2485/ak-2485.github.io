Ariel Kellison is a Senior Research Engineer at Code Metal, where she applies her expertise at the intersection of programming languages, formal verification, and numerical analysis to the development of LLM-based transpilers. She received her Ph.D. in computer science from Cornell University in 2024 and subsequently held a postdoctoral appointment at Sandia National Laboratories. From 2020 to 2024, Ariel was a Department of Energy (DOE) Computational Science Graduate Fellow, and in 2025 she was named a Frederick A. Howes Scholar in Computational Science, one of the DOE's highest honors for emerging leaders in the field. She holds a Bachelor of Science with honors in astrophysics from the University of California, Santa Cruz.

Ariel can be reached by email at ak2485@cornell.edu.
