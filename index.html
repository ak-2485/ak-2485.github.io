My goal is to help people build reliable numerical software. I build foundational tools with rigorous guarantees that make it convenient for programmers to reason about numerical accuracy and correctness. As part of the VeriNum research group, I maintain the VCFloat and LAProof libraries for the Coq Proof Assistant.
